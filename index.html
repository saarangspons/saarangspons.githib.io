Saarang is the annual cultural festival of Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. Accredited with heritage stretching from the late 70s, Saarang has grown to be the largest student-run college festival in the country, with ISO 9001:2008 certification. With a footfall of over 50,00 and attracting demographic diversity that very few can boast of, Saarang remains an unrivaled platform for showcasing the enthusiasm and creativity of the youth.
